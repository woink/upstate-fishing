# Upstate NY Fly Shops

## Catskills Region

### Beaverkill Angler
- **Location:** Roscoe, NY ("Trout Town USA")
- **Website:** https://beaverkillangler.com
- **Services:** Full service shop, guides, instruction, rentals
- **Rivers:** Beaverkill, Willowemoc, Delaware
- **Fishing Report:** https://beaverkillangler.com/fishingreport.html ⭐
- **Notes:** Primary source for current conditions

### Baxter House River Outfitters
- **Location:** Roscoe, NY
- **Website:** https://baxterhouse.net
- **Services:** Guides, lodging, flies
- **Rivers:** Upper Delaware, Beaverkill, Willowemoc
- **Notes:** 25+ years in business, detailed fishing reports on site

### Catskill Outfitters
- **Location:** Phoenicia, NY
- **Website:** https://catskilloutfitters.com
- **Services:** Fly shop, guides, lessons, camping/hiking gear
- **Rivers:** Esopus Creek, Beaverkill, Delaware
- **Notes:** Good for Esopus Creek intel

### Trout Town Flies
- **Location:** Roscoe area
- **Website:** https://trouttownflies.com
- **Services:** Flies, guides
- **Rivers:** Beaverkill, Willowemoc, East & West Delaware, Neversink

### Hackett Fly Fishing
- **Location:** Catskills
- **Website:** https://hackettflyfishing.com
- **Services:** Walk/wade & drift boat trips
- **Rivers:** Beaverkill, Willowemoc, Upper Delaware

## Museums / Resources

### Catskill Fly Fishing Center & Museum
- **Location:** Livingston Manor, NY
- **Website:** https://cffcm.com
- **Notes:** Sells physical hatch charts, historical context
